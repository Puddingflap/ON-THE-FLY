Founder

Ekrem Doğan's vision begins with the future of human health.

OID is led by Ekrem Doğan, founder, CEO, and visionary of the project.

He believes the diseases modern medicine still calls incurable should not remain beyond hope forever. Diagnosis should become faster, treatment should become more personal, rare diseases should no longer be ignored, and patients should not be left alone inside slow, fragmented, expensive systems.

Under Ekrem's leadership, OID is being shaped into a Turkish-born medical intelligence movement: a project that brings together artificial intelligence, medical science, patient data, global research, treatment discovery, and future clinical access into one living system.

For Ekrem, this is the real meaning of utility. Real utility is saving lives.

Born in Türkiye and reaching from Istanbul to the world, Ekrem's vision carries a national spirit with global ambition. He believes Turkish intelligence, ambition, and scientific courage can help shape the next chapter of medicine.

The economic model follows the mission. Profits from OID's AI medical products, health services, and future international clinic model are intended to buy and burn the OID token. But the burn is not the purpose. It is the consequence of the purpose being fulfilled.
